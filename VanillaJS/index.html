Getting Started

All efficient home services packages are designed to help you give a little back to our environment as well As your bank account. With our flexible payment options and highest-rated local service, it’s never been better to go solar in the United States and we make the process easy as can be.

1. IN-HOME CONSULTATION

We’ll visit your home to walk you through the solar process and pricing options, and answer any questions you might have. Free of cost and no obligation.

2. SITE ASSESSMENT

Our in-house engineers return to measure your roof and level of sun exposure and develop a custom solar design tailored to your home.

3. SYSTEM AGREEMENT

We crunch the data from your site assessment and share the final numbers, with clear expectations of what to expect for performance and price. With approval, we’ll get to work on your install!

4. DESIGN & PERMITTING

Our engineers finalize your architectural and electrical plans and we’ll apply for all needed building and electrical permits to install your system. We’ll also reserve your rebates and coordinate with your utility company.

5. INSTALLATION & INSPECTIONS

Installation usually takes just a day for our professional installers! From there we’ll handle your electrical inspection, including a quality check and final walk-through before we notify your utility company and activate your system.
